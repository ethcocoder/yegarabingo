How to Play

1. Choose your stake (10 or 20 ETB)

2. You receive a random 5×5 bingo cartela

3. Numbers are called one at a time (e.g. B-7, I-22)

4. Find the number on your cartela and tap to mark it

5. Complete a row, column, or diagonal to win!

6. Prize = Stake × 15.2 (e.g., 10 ETB → 152 ETB)

* Each round lasts 180 seconds. Play responsibly.
